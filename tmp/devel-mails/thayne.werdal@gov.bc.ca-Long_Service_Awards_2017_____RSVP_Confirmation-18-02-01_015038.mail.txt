* Need to Make Changes or Cancel Attendance? *

Please notify us (longserviceawards@gov.bc.ca [1]) prior to your ceremony
date if you need to cancel your attendance or make any changes to your
information.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
If you have any questions relating to travel arrangements, pre-receptions, or
reimbursement, please visit our website [2] or connnect with your workplace
LSA contact [3].  If your questions are related to the LSA ceremonies at
Government House, please contact longserviceawards@gov.bc.ca [4].

*Please Note:*

The LSA ceremonies take place at Government House [5], the home of the
Lieutenant Governor of British Columbia. It is recommended that you “dress
your best” for these formal, evening occasions and, in consideration of
others, *please refrain from wearing heavily scented products.*

*Itinerary:*

  * 5:45 – 6:15 p.m.- Registration, Reception and Welcome
  * 6:15 – 7:15 p.m.- Dinner Buffet
  * 7:30 – 7:50 p.m.- Speeches
  * 7:50 – 8:30 p.m.- Awards Presentation
  * 8:15 p.m.- Dessert Buffet
  * 9:00 – 10:00 p.m.- Dancing
  * 10:00 p.m. - Evening Concludes

We look forward to welcoming you to your LSA ceremony this fall.
Congratulations on reaching this important milestone in your career!

Long Service Awards Team
